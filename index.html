About Me

My name is Sofia Echavarria and I am a senior in the College of Engineering at Cornell University majoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering. I am graduating in December 2024. I am planning on continuing my work for Arcadis in Denver, Colorado once I graduate.

My professional and academic interests lie in the intersection between elevtrical and environmental engineering. I have seen the need for electrical engineers in environmental remediation projects when managing sensors, developing technology deployment plans, data management and troubleshooting any electronics implementation. In my experience working with RMS Energy Consulting and Arcadis, both environmental engineering consulting companies, I have been able to help with testing, development and implementation of a variety of emerging technology to support various environmental remediation efforts.
